About The Author

My name is Robert Kobrin, and I've had the pleasure of competing in FTC robotics for Team 731 Wannabee Strange for the past two years, and I feel like I have learned a lot about robotics design in this time.

As a member of Wannabee Strange, I have had the pleasure of winning several awards. These include the following:

  • North Carolina Inspire Award 2019
  • World Championship Design Award Finalist 2019
  • North Carolina Design Award 2020
