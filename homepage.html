Robert Kobrin Robotics

Introduction

Hi! My name is Robert, and I competed on FIRST Tech Challenge Team 731 for two years as a part of the mech and design team. Through this website, I hope to document as much of the knowledge that I have gained through two years of dedication as possible. I hope you enjoy it! Please feel free to contact me with any questions or suggestions that you may have.

About Me

I joined FTC 731 Wannabee Strange at the start of my junior year in high school after watching my friends compete at the state competition the previous year. I quickly became immersed in robotics, and I ended up captain of the team my senior year. My junior year, I was on the mech team and I served as a driver. We were on the finalist alliance at the North Carolina state championship, ranked top 60 in the world, and were a Worlds design award finalist.

My senior year, I served as Team Captain and Design Lead for the team. While the World championship was cancelled, we were the design award winners at the North Carolina state championship.
